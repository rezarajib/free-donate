SHELTER FOR A FAMILY

Through contracts with service providers, the Department of Human Services (DHS) offers emergency shelter to families experiencing homelessness in safe and dignified buildings through the District. Each program provides a rich array of supports and service connections including housing search, connections to mental health and employment services, and meaningful activities for childrenwith the goal of helping families exit to permanent housing and get back on their feet quickly.
